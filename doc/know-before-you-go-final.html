Frequently Asked Questions

Can I re-enter the museum after leaving?

No. Your ticket allows a single entry only. Once you exit the museum, you cannot re-enter with the same ticket.

Is a physical guide included?

No, a physical guide is not included with your booking. You are welcome to use our online guide or the PopGuide audio experience if included in your booking.

Can I change my date or time?

Unfortunately, your entry date and time are fixed once confirmed. Changes cannot be made. Entry is only valid for the specific date and time selected.

Are strollers and baby items allowed?

Yes. Baby food, baby bottles, powdered milk, and small fruit juice boxes (under 250 ml) are permitted for children. Highchairs are available in the cafeteria on request. Children may drink from bottles, cups, or small bottles with non-drip closures.

Can I propose in the museum?

Yes! The Uffizi welcomes marriage proposals on the premises, including the cafeteria. However, this must not disturb other visitors, and no professional photographers or equipment are permitted. Entrance with ceremonial clothing or accessories is not allowed.

Where can I find more official information?

Visit the Uffizi Gallery official FAQ page at uffizi.it/en/pages/faq for complete and up-to-date information.
